About Me

Built for
construction business

I'm Rose Dorainne Marquez, the founder of RDigitals Executive & Bookkeeping Services. I work with small construction, renovation, real estate, and pool building businesses — the kind run by busy owners who need a reliable, organized right hand.

My work sits at the intersection of executive assistance and bookkeeping: keeping your calendar clear, your documents in order, your invoices sent, and your numbers tracked — so you can focus on the job site, not the inbox.

I've worked with real paying clients across the US, EU, and AU as an independent contractor, bringing a specialist tool stack and construction-industry know-how that most general VAs simply don't have.
